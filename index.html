A Wake-Up Call: Safety Can’t Wait

In light of the recent heartbreaking near-drowning incident that’s been making headlines, it’s a wake-up call for all hotels and pool operators.

Having lifeguards on duty is insufficient if they aren’t trained to identify drowning before it happens — especially when children are left unsupervised by adults.

At Park Royal Hotel Melaka, we recently conducted a focused lifeguard training session. Even though the pool is shallow, the management didn’t take safety lightly.

Lifeguards were equipped with real-life rescue skills and, more importantly, trained to spot the early signs of drowning — often silent and easy to miss.

We commend Park Royal for going beyond just compliance — they chose prevention.
